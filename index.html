Our Story

Founded in 2001 by the Sharma family, Bella Vista has been serving authentic Indian cuisine for over two decades.

Our love for traditional Indian cooking, combined with contemporary culinary techniques, creates a truly memorable dining experience. We source the freshest produce and spices from local farmers and hand-select specialty ingredients from across India to ensure every dish is packed with genuine flavor.

From our aromatic biryanis to our hand-prepared breads and vibrant curries, every meal is crafted with care and respect for Indian culinary traditions. Our head chef, Rajiv Sharma, trained in the kitchens of Lucknow and brings generations of rich family recipes and regional specialties to your table, blending the depth and diversity of Indian cuisine with modern creativity.
